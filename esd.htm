The department's mission is to provide technology development and engineering support to the Group's steel division and associated steel mills in the region.

Our capabilities and range of services include sourcing, procurement, feasibility studies,  plant layout, equipment and facilities design/development, project management/execution including erection and commissioning of  new plants, upgrading and revamping of existing plants, facility maintenance/operation and consultancy services to improve manufacturing competitiveness, customer satisfaction, productivity, quality, safety, health and environmental standards.

The department has completed numerous projects both locally and overseas. Notable ones include:

1. Revamping of an existing bar/wire rod mill for Singapore Steel Division.

2. Erection of  a new 5-strands continuous casting machine for Singapore Steel Division.

3. Erection of a bar/wire rod mill for NatSteel Vina, Vietnam.

4. Erection of a new bar and wire rod mill for Southern NatSteel (Xiamen), People Republic of China.

5. Erection of a new 80 Ton Electric Arc Shaft Furnace c/w Ladle Furnace for Singapore Steel Division.

6. Erection of a new bar and wire rod mill for Singapore Steel Division.
